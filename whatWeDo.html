What We Do

Now, we all know that looking for a new office space is a real headache!​

Flux is here to make the experience not only fun and enjoyable, but completely hassle free. So much so, that you will be able to focus on your day job, without the stress of “okay, we really need to call back all of those office providers and book in viewings”.

Flux's service is completely free to their clients.

Flux receive an industry-standard fee from the office provider, once the client has secured a new home.
