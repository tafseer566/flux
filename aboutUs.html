About Us

Flux is not your average office agency, brokerage or consultancy (otherwise they would have named themselves something boring and self-explanatory just like that).
Flux instead is named after the situation in which a lot of their customers are in. Whether it be upsizing, or downsizing, their headcount and business may well be in a state of Flux, and need the support of an expert office broker to guide them through the minefield that is the serviced office market.

Experts in the Flex industry, Flux is a cutting-edge, modern start-up, helping companies of all shapes and sizes throughout their journey. Flux help businesses evolve, and support their growth as they adapt to change, good or bad. Flux is a start-up, helping start-ups. Flux does this by sourcing their clients the best possible office space, at zero cost.
